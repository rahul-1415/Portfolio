Gaming

Gaming is love, Gaming is life

My “hobby” is gaming. I usually don’t use the term hobby, but I play quite often. Gaming is my way to relax. I spend my free time playing games online with my friends and it’s the way I connect with my friends. Gaming means to me a lot. I think could not live without my pc! My inspiration in gaming begun when I got a Ps2 for my birthday present a long time ago. My favourite games are League of Legends, Counter Strike Global Offensive, Warframe, Fallout franchise and Skyrim.


The pros

Gaming is a great time killer! The pros of gaming are quite few, but there are some. Your reflexes might improve if you play games that need timing. You learn English while you play because games are usually in English. You can make new friends while playing. Gaming is really fun and satisfying.


The cons

Gaming can be addictive! The cons of gaming are not that bad, it depends on how much you play and from what point you look at it. You can spend long times playing games and that will get you out of shape. You can also lose the interest to do homework and such. If gaming becomes addictive, it might affect your relationships and social life outside of gaming. Gaming costs money. Gaming can be really cost an arm if you plan to start playing on a computer. Many new games cost 60 € when they come out. New gaming consoles cost about 400 € and if you plan to build a good gaming computer, it is going to much more.


Recommendations

If you plan on not playing so much, I recommend buying a console. If you plan on playing big time, I suggest you save money to build yourself a gaming Pc. A gaming Pc is just so much more superior than a console. On a Pc you have millions of games that you can download free from internet.
